Ryan International Schools are a group of private educational institutions in India and the UAE and other countries as well. It was founded in 1976 by Dr. Augustine Francis Pinto. According to a report by News 18 in September 2017, the group employs over 18,000 faculty members, and over 30,000 students pass from Ryan schools annually. Students of Ryan International Schools are commonly known as Ryanites.
